Why This Matters

SoundExchange only collects US digital-radio royalties. Public performance of your recordings abroad is collected by foreign societies — but only if you're registered with them (directly or via an agent). Most independent artists never register internationally and leave this income uncollected.

SoundExchange status and collected income are from your catalog. International coverage and uncollected figures are estimates.
