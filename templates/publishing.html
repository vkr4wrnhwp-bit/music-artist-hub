How Publishing Pays

Performance royalties

Earned when your song is played publicly (radio, streaming, venues). Collected by your PRO — ASCAP, BMI, or SESAC.

Mechanical royalties

Earned per reproduction/stream in the US. Collected by The MLC — but only for works registered with them.

Mechanical and uncollected figures are estimates; PRO/MLC status and performance income are from your catalog.
